Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the FitRoute Pro movement

What happens when we reach 10,000 signatures? +

When we reach our goal of 10,000 signatures from Gauteng runners, we will immediately begin development of Gauteng-specific features and prioritize the region for our beta launch. All signatories will receive early access.

How much will FitRoute Pro cost? +

There will be a free version with basic features and a premium subscription (approximately R99/month) with advanced analytics, personalized training plans, and exclusive features. Movement supporters will receive special lifetime pricing.

Is my data secure? +

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. We comply with POPIA regulations and will never sell your personal data. Your running routes and activity data belong to you. Read our detailed Privacy Policy for more information.

What makes this different from other running apps? +

FitRoute Pro focuses specifically on route discovery and creation with AI technology, community-driven content, and features designed for the South African running environment. We integrate with popular platforms but offer unique tools for route planning and local community engagement.
